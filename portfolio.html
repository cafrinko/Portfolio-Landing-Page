About Catherine

Catherine is an aspiring user experience and interaction designer. With a love for the intersections of art and technology, she hopes to design educational journeys that engage users through aesthetically stunning interactive art installations. To this end, she recently began attending Epicodus, a socially conscious code school in Portland, Oregon that values and respects the differences among people. When she finishes her program in September of 2016, she will be relocating to Berlin to become immersed in their flourishing art/tech culture.

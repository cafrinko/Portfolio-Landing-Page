About Catherine

Catherine is an aspiring user experience and interaction designer. She has a wide-ranging educational background in visual art, environmental engineering, and holistic public health. Included in her myriad of professional interests is a love for the intersections of art and technology, she hopes to design educational journeys that engage users through aesthetically stunning interactive art installations. To this end, she recently began attending Epicodus, a socially conscious code school in Portland, Oregon that values and respects the differences among people. When she finishes her program in September of 2016, she will be relocating to Berlin to become immersed in their flourishing art/tech culture.

Catherine's personal interests involve keeping abreast of social and environmental justice movements, reading fiction and poetic nonfiction, preparing food, nature walks, bicycling, and juggling. Though a jack-of-all-trades, she strives to become a master of all of them.
